Help Section

Need help with getting the screensaver's or channels installed on your Roku Device please follow the installation below.

Install Roku Screensaver:

  • Power on your Roku device
  • Navigate to the search section on the menu
  • In search box Type in the name of the screensaver you are looking for.
  • Slide over to the final screen and select get screensaver.
  • Method #2 Go to the Roku section of our site and click add to Roku and it will take you to the Roku channel store website and you click install.
  • To apply the screensaver go to settings on the Roku device and select the screensaver you want and click set primary screensaver.
